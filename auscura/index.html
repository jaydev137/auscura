About Auscura

Revolutionizing Respiratory Diagnostics

Auscura is an intelligent diagnostic platform that integrates biomedical engineering and artificial intelligence to assist in early detection of respiratory disorders.

The system analyzes lung auscultation sounds acquired from an electronic stethoscope, using advanced signal processing techniques and machine learning models to classify diseases such as Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD), Asthma, and Pneumonia, along with recognizing Healthy lung function.

The ultimate goal of Auscura is to create an accessible web-based diagnostic tool that connects to an IoT-enabled stethoscope for real-time screening, helping clinicians and patients perform early respiratory assessments remotely.

Respiratory Conditions We Detect

Advanced AI analysis for common respiratory diseases

COPD

Chronic inflammation causing airflow blockage and breathing difficulty

Asthma

Recurrent airway narrowing triggered by allergens or exertion

Pneumonia

Infection causing fluid-filled air sacs and impaired gas exchange

Healthy

Normal, smooth, and consistent breath sounds without abnormalities
